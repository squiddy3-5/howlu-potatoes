# 🥔🌙 HOWLU POTATOES — WORLD DOCUMENT

---

# 🌍 WORLD OVERVIEW

The world of Howlu Potatoes was once a stable magical ecosystem powered by the **Potato Moon (Mopo)**.

This era was known as:

> 🥔 The Golden Mash Age

A time of:

* floating settlements
* living potato magic
* stable memory-based reality
* low conflict between regions

---

# 💥 THE FALL OF THE WORLD

To prevent an unknown collapse, **Boat Mom** created the **Starch Engine System**.

Instead of saving the world, it caused fragmentation:

* The **Mopo (Moon Potato)** was pulled into the world
* regions became disconnected and unstable
* pathways between places stopped behaving consistently
* creatures formed from corrupted starch energy
* memory became something that could change or conflict

This event is called:

> ⚓ The Starch Engine Incident

---

# ⚓ BOAT MOM

Boat Mom is the central war figure of the modern world.

### Motivation:

She believes:

> the world is already collapsing, and only total control can prevent extinction.

She leads:

> ⚓ The Armada

### Philosophy:

* Chaos = destruction
* Control = survival
* uncontrolled systems = failure

She creates structured “recipes of war” rather than natural life.

---

# 🍲 PAELLA MONSTERS

Engineered combat beings created by Boat Mom.

Formed from:

* starch energy
* Mopo fragments
* “recipe logic” (structured combat design)

## Types:

### 🔥 Sear Units

Frontline destruction units that burn terrain and suppress resistance.

### 🛡️ Broth Guardians

Defensive units that absorb damage and stabilize battlefield zones.

### 🌫️ Steam Mimics

Infiltration units that:

* copy NPCs
* copy player behavior
* infiltrate factions

### 🧠 Cooked Minds

Semi-aware failed recipes that sometimes:

* question orders
* regain fragments of identity
* break combat logic briefly

---

# ⚔️ FACTIONS

## 🥔 SPUD WARDENS

Goal: restore balance and heal the world.

Belief:

> The Mopo must be stabilized, not controlled.

They work to:

* reconnect unstable regions
* protect natural life
* preserve the original state of the world

---

## ⚓ BOAT MOM’S ARMADA

Goal: unify and control the world.

Belief:

> structure prevents collapse.

They:

* enforce order across regions
* deploy Paella Monsters
* attempt to control pathways and territory

---

## 🧠 THE ARCHIVISTS

Goal: preserve all memory and truth.

Belief:

> Nothing should be lost, even if it contradicts.

They emerged after the Starch Engine Incident when memory itself became unstable.

They:

* record events, choices, and alternate outcomes
* track changes in the world and the player
* preserve conflicting versions of reality rather than resolving them

Some Archivists believe that:

> understanding every version of truth is more important than choosing one.

---

# 🧓 ROOT HOME

A neutral zone outside faction control.

Used when:

* player faints
* connection to the world breaks
* the Mopo system re-stabilizes the player

It is not a place in the normal sense, but a point of recovery between states.

---

# 🧓 ROOT KEEPER

## 🌱 Early Role:

* guide
* lore explainer
* neutral observer
* reacts to world changes

---

## 🔗 CONNECTION TO PLAYER

The Root Keeper is directly linked to the Mopo system.

He can:

* sense player actions
* remember past choices
* retain information even when the world shifts

---

## 🧠 DYNAMIC DIALOGUE

Changes based on:

* faction alignment
* faint count
* lore collected
* world instability
* remembered player actions

---

## 🌙 FINAL FORM — ROOT ARCHIVE

A merged structure of:

* all memories
* all recorded events
* all conflicting truths

Goal:

> unify reality into a single consistent interpretation

Conflict:

> doing so would erase alternative outcomes and player freedom

---

# 🧭 PLAYER SYSTEM

Character selection is currently a starting choice.

Later it may evolve into:

* identity types
* faction-linked forms
* memory-based manifestations of the player

---

# 💀 HP SYSTEM — FAINT MECHANIC

When HP reaches 0:

The player does not die.

They:

* lose connection to the current region
* are pulled into Root Home by the system

The Root Keeper restores them.

---

# 🏠 ROOT HOME RECOVERY

* player wakes up safely
* HP restored partially or fully
* world state may shift slightly
* faint count increases

---

---

## ⚓ ARMADA ENDING

---

# 🧠 CORE IDEA

The world is shaped by memory, interpretation, and the unstable influence of the Mopo system.

Different factions attempt to define reality in different ways:

* preserve it
* control it
* or record all versions of it
