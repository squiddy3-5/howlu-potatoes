# ⚔️ COMBAT SYSTEM (CORE DESIGN)

Combat is not just damage-based — it is interpretation-based warfare.

---

## 🧠 MEMORY STACK

Tracks combat history:

* repeated moves become stronger OR unstable
* overuse causes distortion effects
* abilities may evolve or corrupt over time

---

## 🔄 ACTION SYSTEM

Each turn, player chooses:

* Basic Attack
* Ability
* Defend
* Observe (reveals enemy patterns + boosts Archivist alignment)

---

## ⚖️ ALIGNMENT IMPACT IN COMBAT

🥔 WARDEN:

* healing skills stronger
* defense scaling increases
* enemies become less chaotic

⚓ ARMADA:

* higher burst damage
* structured combo chains unlock
* enemies counter predictable patterns

🧠 ARCHIVIST:

* ability duplication possible
* echo attacks repeat past moves
* random truth effects

---

## 🧠 MEMORY DISTORTION MECHANIC

If a move is repeated too often:

* it may evolve
* or glitch into a new version
* or backfire depending on faction alignment

Example:

* Water Blast → Overwritten Surge
* or Corrupted Wave

---

## ☠️ BREAK STATE (LOW HP + HIGH DISTORTION)

* UI may glitch visually
* enemies may lose turn logic
* abilities may rename or mutate mid-combat
* Root Keeper commentary may appear
